Welcome to Saiphia Casting

Your Go-To Experts for All Casting Needs.

At Saiphia Casting, we specialize in crafting precision ferrous and non-ferrous castings, ranging from 25 gms to 450 Kg pieces. Employing cutting-edge sand and lost wax technology, we ensure the production of intricate precision components. Our commitment to quality is underscored by a state-of-the-art QC lab, featuring diverse testing facilities. Additionally, our heat treatment furnace, equipped with carburizing capabilities, further enhances the excellence of our castings."
